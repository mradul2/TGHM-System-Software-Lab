About Us

Hello! We are the members of the team "Kiddo and Frens". TGHM was designed by our group as a part of our final project for the course "Software Engineering Laboratory" at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. The software has been created with the motive of acting as a middleman between the railways management and the food service providers, and to provide a platform for the customers to order food with ease, while on rail journeys.
